Dr. Miaoguang Lin — Dr.Ac; T.C.M

Dr. Lin is a registered acupuncturist and Traditional Chinese Medicine practitioner licensed in Saskatchewan and Canada. With over 34 years of clinical experience, he combines classical TCM knowledge with a rigorous medical background.

He graduated from a Chinese medical university in 1990 and spent years as a trauma surgeon in municipal hospitals across Guangdong Province before dedicating his practice entirely to TCM and acupuncture.

Dr. Lin established his Saskatoon clinic in 2015 and has since served patients from across Saskatchewan seeking natural, evidence-informed care.
